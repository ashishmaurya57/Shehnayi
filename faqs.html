How to register in Shehnayi.com?

Registration on Shehnayi.com is Free and easy. You just need to follow the instructions and fill your personal details.

How to add profile pictures?

When you fill your details under Registration form, moving further you will get a page to upload your profile picture. There you can upload your pictures.

Can we change our profile picture after some time duration?

Yes, you can change your profile picture anytime.

How to search for matches?

When you login, your profile page (user panel) will open, and you will be able to see matches on the dashboard itself.

Absolutely, your details are safe with us.

After registration, do I get a membership ID?

Yes, after you register and fill the details in the registration form, you will see your membership Id as SHXXXX.

Can we shortlist the profiles?

There is a button named shortlist. If you like anyone you can move that person's profile in the shortlist option by clicking on the button of shortlist.

How can I show my interest to the person if I like someone?

You will see a send/receive interest button at the bottom right side of the profile by clicking that you can send/receive interest.

How do I send a message or contact to the person in whom I am interested or other is interested in me?

You can see a chat bot button available at the bottom right side, you can even initiate the chat with the person you like.
